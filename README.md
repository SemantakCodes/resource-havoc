# Resource Havoc: Multiplayer Sandbox

A cooperative multiplayer sandbox experience where teamwork is the only way to win. You and a friend are dropped into a vast environment with one goal: gather the required resources and secure your victory.

## 🎮 Gameplay
In **Resource Havoc**, communication is your most valuable asset. Explore the sandbox, manage your tools, and coordinate with your partner to find rare materials hidden across the map.

* **Cooperative Play:** Designed specifically for 2-player interaction.
* **Sandbox Exploration:** No set path—find your own way to gather what you need.
* **Resource Management:** Collect, store, and refine materials to meet the winning criteria.
